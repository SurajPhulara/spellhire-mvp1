## üîÑ **CORE WORKFLOW SYSTEMS**

### **Application Processing Workflow**
The complete application process begins with AI-powered job discovery that matches positions to candidate profiles based on comprehensive compatibility analysis. Candidates submit applications using pre-filled information from their profiles, triggering automated screening that evaluates basic qualifications against job requirements.

Qualified candidates enter the recruiter's visual pipeline management system, progressing through customizable hiring stages with real-time notifications at each transition. Built-in messaging facilitates direct communication between candidates and recruiters throughout the process.

The persistent scoring system ensures that all feedback and ratings improve candidate profiles for future applications, while final hiring decisions trigger automated notifications to all remaining candidates in the pipeline.

### **Intelligent Matching Algorithm**
The matching system evaluates multiple weighted factors to determine candidate-job compatibility. Skills analysis represents the highest weight factor at 80%, comparing required versus candidate skills with proficiency level matching. Experience alignment considers years of relevant experience against position requirements, while location compatibility evaluates remote, onsite, and hybrid work preferences.

Salary expectation matching compares candidate requirements with offered compensation ranges. Career progression analysis evaluates growth opportunity alignment, while company culture assessment matches values and work style preferences. Historical performance data from previous interviews and success rates inform matching decisions, and persistent scoring incorporates candidate reputation and reliability metrics.

**Discover Colleagues Portal**: Advanced employer discovery system enabling employers to search and connect with colleagues across the organization. Features include upward reporting hierarchy visualization (DFS traversal), team context display, and permission-based visibility controls. Admins see full company hierarchy, HR sees organizational scope, managers see their teams plus upward reporting lines, and employers see their immediate hierarchy and peer network.

**My Team Portal**: Comprehensive team management system for managers and HR professionals. Provides complete subgraph visualization of direct and indirect reports through downward DFS traversal. Includes team analytics dashboard with headcount trends, department distribution, skills heatmaps, and performance metrics. Supports bulk team operations including meeting scheduling, performance reviews, and goal setting.

**Department Management**: Full organizational structure management with department creation, hierarchy establishment, and leadership assignment. Features include department head assignment with professional titles (Head, Manager, Director, Lead, Supervisor), parent-child department relationships, budget tracking, and employer assignment capabilities. Supports department merging, archiving, and analytics.

**Interactive Org Chart**: Auto-generated organizational charts built from department and reporting manager relationships. Features drag-and-drop reorganization, expandable/collapsible team nodes, profile integration, and export capabilities (PNG/SVG). Updates automatically when employer details change and respects permission-based visibility.

### **Candidate-Centered Features**
Candidates benefit from AI-powered profile optimization suggestions that improve visibility and matching success. Smart job matching provides personalized recommendations that improve continuously through machine learning. Comprehensive application tracking offers real-time status updates across all submitted applications.

Career insight analytics help candidates understand application success patterns and identify improvement opportunities. Skills development recommendations highlight gaps and growth areas, while portfolio integration connects GitHub, LinkedIn, and personal website profiles. Persistent scoring enables reputation building over time through consistent performance.

### **Advanced Communication Systems**
**Group Chat Architecture**: Comprehensive group communication system with strict entity separation. Candidate groups support study groups, industry discussions, location-based networking, and skill-based communities. Employer groups enable department-specific channels, project collaboration, cross-functional discussions, and knowledge sharing within organizations.

**Entity Separation Rules**: Strict separation between candidate and employer groups ensures privacy and security. Candidates can only create or join groups with other candidates. Employer members can only create or join groups with other employer from their own company. Cross-company employer groups are prohibited, and mixed groups (candidates and employer together) are strictly forbidden.

**Enhanced Messaging System**: Sophisticated messaging architecture supporting two distinct thread types with strict recruiter-controlled initiation. Job-specific threads are tied to applications and created when recruiters message about specific positions, with automatic thread titles including job context. General recruitment threads are for potential future roles with 5-day response windows and auto-closure if candidates don't respond. Thread management includes automatic creation only when recruiters send first messages, time-bound communication rules, and intelligent closure based on hiring decisions. Candidates cannot initiate conversations with recruiters - all messaging is recruiter-controlled to maintain professional boundaries and prevent spam.

### **AI-Powered Intelligence**
The intelligent matching system achieves over 90% accuracy in job-candidate pairing through sophisticated algorithms that analyze multiple compatibility factors. Automated screening reduces manual review time by 80% while maintaining quality standards. Predictive analytics forecast hiring success and candidate fit probability based on historical data.

Natural language processing extracts skills and requirements from job descriptions automatically. Bias reduction algorithms help identify and mitigate unconscious bias in hiring decisions. The persistent learning system continuously improves matching accuracy through feedback incorporation and pattern recognition.

### **Advanced Permission & Access Control System**
**Role-Based Architecture**: Sophisticated permission system with four primary roles and granular access controls. Admin role provides full system access including all data, settings, pipelines, org charts, reports, and audit logs. HR role manages organizational hierarchy, employer records, and department structure within their scope. employer role offers basic self-service access with limited team visibility.

**Recruiter Add-On Permission**: Recruiter is not a standalone role but an additional permission that can be granted to any primary role (Admin, HR, or employer). Recruiters can create and manage job postings, assign interviewers, update candidate stages, view recruitment metrics, and initiate messages to candidates. However, they cannot modify employer hierarchy, which requires Admin or HR permissions.

**Granular Feature Access**: Each role has specific access to platform features. Admins can access Discover Colleagues Portal (full company hierarchy) and My Team Portal (all company subgraphs). HR can access full company hierarchy and their teams plus upward reporting lines. employers can access their own hierarchy plus immediate peers and manager chain. Department management permissions vary by role with Admins having full control and HR having scope-limited access.

**Entity Separation & Privacy**: Strict separation between candidate and employer profiles ensures data security. Employer profiles are invisible to candidates, and candidates cannot search for or view employer information. Cross-company employer visibility is prohibited, and all communications respect entity boundaries with appropriate permission checks.

### **Comprehensive User Onboarding System**
**Multi-Step Profile Completion**: Rigorous onboarding process ensures complete user profiles before platform access. Candidates must complete personal information, professional details, skills & experience, and job preferences sections with 100% completion required. Employer users must complete both company profile setup (first) and personal profile setup (second) before gaining dashboard access.

**Access Control During Onboarding**: Platform features are blocked until profile completion to maintain data quality and matching accuracy. Candidates cannot access dashboard, job search, applications, or messaging until profile is 100% complete. Employer users cannot access dashboard, job posting, or team management until both company and personal profiles are complete.

**Progressive Profile Building**: Step-by-step guided experience with progress tracking, auto-save functionality, and validation at each stage. Real-time progress bars show completion percentage, and users receive clear guidance on required fields and missing information. Profile completion triggers AI matching algorithm processing and job recommendation generation.

**Onboarding Email Automation**: Automated email sequences guide users through the onboarding process with clear next steps, login credentials, and platform access instructions. Employer members receive temporary passwords and profile completion links, while candidates get verification emails and profile setup guidance.
